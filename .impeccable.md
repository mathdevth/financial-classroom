## Design Context

### Users
High school students (aged 13-18) in Thailand. Their goal is to track their financial learning progress and view past simulation results as achievements.

### Brand Personality
Premium, Inspiring, Professional.

### Aesthetic Direction
Premium Light-Mode Glassmorphism. Since we are moving towards a "Snowy Glass" (White/Light) theme for better readability, the modal should have a translucent white background, soft shadows, and colorful accents for each module.

### Accessibility
High contrast for text readability, clear iconography, and a clean layout suitable for mobile and desktop screens.

### Design Principles
- Flagship-level polish: Every detail matters, flagship not MVP.
- Glassmorphism: Translucent, layered, soft, and premium.
- Colorful accents: Each module has a distinct color for quick recognition.
- Responsive: Works beautifully on mobile and desktop.
- Accessible: High contrast, readable, and clear for all users.
